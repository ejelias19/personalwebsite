One example of something I used to do is play basketball. I found a love for the sport when I was 5 years old and since then, I have spent much of my time developing my game.

Coming into Damien High School, the basketball team had proven to be one of the most formidable teams in the entire state.

Damien's basketball team had won the Division III state championship against Campolindo. Expectations had been high for the incoming class at Damien High School. Unfortunately, by the time I was a freshman, the head coach, Matt Dunn, left to work at St. John Bosco.
